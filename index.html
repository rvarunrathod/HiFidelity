Powerful Features

Everything you need for the ultimate music listening experience

Professional Audio

Uncompromised Sound Quality

Powered by the BASS audio library, HiFidelity delivers studio-quality playback. Support for FLAC, WAV, AIFF, and more ensures you hear every detail exactly as the artist intended.

Mini Player

Always with you

The Mini Player provides a compact view of your current track and playback controls. Keep your music accessible without cluttering your workspace.

Lyrics

Sing along

Real-time, time-synced lyrics synchronized with your playback. Powered by lrclib with automatic downloads built-in, so you never miss a word.

Search

Find it fast

Lightning-fast full-text search across your entire library. Find any song, album, or artist instantly, no matter how large your collection.

Queue

Intelligent Auto-Play

Smart recommendations based on your listening habits keep the music flowing. Never worry about what to play next.

Equalizer

Tune it your way

Multi-band equalizer with presets allows you to fine-tune frequencies to match your headphones and personal preferences.
